More about me!

Background

I was born on January 6th, 1999 in Ulsan, South Korea and my family moved to the States in 2006. Thanks to my family, I have retained Korean culture and the language, so I am proud to say that I am fluent in both English and Korean.

While I am currently in Ann Arbor, Michigan for school, I have lived in Bergen County, New Jersey ever since moving here! Back in Jersey, I live with my parents, my older sister, my older brother and sister-in-law, and just over a year ago, I was blessed with the most adorable niece, Ellie!.

My inspiration

Family is the single most important and valuable thing to me in life. The word itself brings feelings of warmth and a sense of security. I can always count on them, share my problems, and just be myself at anytime. They always provide me with unconditional love and support through the good and the bad.

Growing up as the youngest I was lucky enough to have received so much love and affection. Family to me encompasses encouragement, understanding, comfort, faith, and so much more. I believe that it's because of them that I am able to reciprocate that love to others, especially to the youngest addition to my family, my baby niece.

A few fun facts

I have two opposite sides to me. I am a big homebody - I love staying in bed solving puzzles, watching Netflix, and playing phone games. At the same time, once I leave the house, I crave action and adventure. I like to go bowling with my friends and many of them have bought bowling shoes because of me.

When I travel with family and friends, I prefer outdoor activities such as hiking, parasailing, jetskiing, etc. to going to museums. My lifelong bucketlist includes bungee jumping, skydiving, and paragliding!
