More about me!

Background

I was born on January 6th, 1999 in Ulsan, South Korea and my family moved to the States in 2006. Thanks to my family, I have retained Korean culture and the language, so I am proud to say that I am fluent in both English and Korean.

While I am currently in Ann Arbor, Michigan for school, I have lived in Bergen County, New Jersey ever since moving here! Back in Jersey, I live with my parents, my older sister, my older brother and sister-in-law, and just over a year ago, I was blessed with the most adorable niece, Ellie! (Many pictures are coming your way ♡ ).

Family

Family is the single most important to me in life. They provide me with unconditional love and support every step of the way. Growing up as the youngest, I received so much love and affection, and I believe that it's because of them that I am able to reciprocate that love to others.

I realize how valuable family is especially when babysitting my baby niece. As much as I love Ellie, I see how much patience it takes to be a parent, and I can't even image what my parents had gone through to raise three kids. Still, at the end of the day, when I see the way my brother and my sister-in-law look at Ellie, I realize that there is nothing more important than family.
